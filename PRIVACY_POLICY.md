# Privacy Policy for KeepIt

Last updated: April 24, 2026

At KeepIt, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## 1. Data Collection
KeepIt is designed as an offline-first application. All data you enter, including:
- Weight entries
- Height, Age, and Gender
- Mood and personal notes
- Achievement progress

...is stored **locally on your device**. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of this data to external servers.

## 2. Permissions
- **Notifications**: Used solely to remind you to log your weight at your requested time.
- **Battery Optimization**: Requested to ensure notifications arrive accurately on some Android devices.

## 3. Data Control
You have full control over your data:
- You can export your data to a CSV file at any time.
- You can delete all your data permanently from the Profile settings.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We do not share your data with any third-party services.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
If we update this policy, we will notify you through an app update.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions, please contact the developer through the Google Play Store contact information.
